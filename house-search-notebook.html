Welcome to House Search Notebook

A private, offline tool guiding you through every stage of finding and buying your home.

📖 What is this?

A structured notebook for house hunters. It will guide you through each stage of your home search journey.

🔒 Your data stays private

Everything you type is auto-saved to your browser's localStorage. No data is ever sent anywhere. The file works completely offline.

🗺️ The sections

  1. Your Values — Reflect on what truly matters to you in a home and neighbourhood.
  2. Countries & Cities — Note which places you are open to living in and why.
  3. Search Filters — Set concrete filters: bedrooms, price range, property types, search areas.
  4. Criteria & Weights — Define what you'll score houses on; assign importance weights.
  5. Rate Houses — Add houses, score each criterion with stars, add per-criterion notes.
  6. Comparison — Bar charts and a score table comparing all your houses.
  7. Mortgage Calculator — Estimate monthly repayments for any combination of price, deposit, rate, and term.
  8. Purchase Checklist — categorised tasklist to track your purchase progress.
